ABOUT US

Inbeauty is a company that offers beauty classes both online and offline. Inbeauty provides complete, exclusive and long-term skill learning material for all levels from beginners to professionals. The mentors that we offer also have experience in the world of Makeup Artist and are certified.

Inbeauty offers free classes for first-time meetings for new participants. We also offer free video access for the first 20 minutes. If visitors are interested in the material offered, visitors can subscribe to videos from Inbeauty, to access the video as a whole.
